## 🗓️ Itinerary
**5-Day Trip to Paris Itinerary**

**Day 1: Arrival and Exploring Montmartre**

* Arrive at Paris airport (note: the provided flight results do not include a direct flight to Paris, so you may need to book a separate flight from Findel, Narita, or another airport to Paris)
* Check-in at Hotel La Comtesse, Hotel Eiffel Seine, or Hotel Metropol, or a popular Airbnb in St Germain or Faubourg-
* Visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica in Montmartre
* Explore the charming streets and alleys of Montmartre, including the Place du Tertre
* Enjoy a traditional French dinner at a local bistro

**Day 2: Iconic Landmarks**

* Start the day with a visit to the Eiffel Tower (book tickets in advance to avoid long wait times)
* Take a scenic river cruise along the Seine to see the city's iconic landmarks, including the Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Louvre Museum
* Visit the Arc de Triomphe and walk along the Champs-Élysées
* Enjoy a picnic lunch in the Luxembourg Gardens
* Spend the evening exploring the Latin Quarter and enjoying the street performers and live music

**Day 3: Museums and Galleries**

* Visit the Louvre Museum (book tickets in advance to avoid long wait times)
* Explore the Musée d'Orsay, which features an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art
* Visit the Musée Rodin, which showcases the works of French sculptor Auguste Rodin
* Enjoy a coffee or snack at a charming café, such as Café de Flore or Les Deux Magots
* Spend the evening enjoying a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge or Lido de Paris

**Day 4: Royal Palaces and Gardens**

* Visit the Palace of Versailles (book tickets in advance to avoid long wait times)
* Explore the stunning gardens and fountain shows
* Visit the Royal Chapel and the Hall of Mirrors
* Return to Paris and enjoy a relaxing stroll in the Tuileries Garden
* Enjoy a dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant, such as Le Grand Vefour or Le Meurice

**Day 5: Markets and Shopping**

* Visit the Marché aux Puces de Saint-Ouen (flea market) for a unique shopping experience
* Explore the historic district of Le Marais, which features trendy boutiques, art galleries, and street food
* Visit the Galeries Lafayette or Le Bon Marché for a luxurious shopping experience
* Enjoy a farewell dinner at a classic Parisian bistro, such as Chez L'Ami Jean or Le Comptoir du Relais

**Additional Tips and Recommendations**

* Consider purchasing a Paris Museum Pass, which grants access to many of the city's top museums and attractions
* Take a cooking class or food tour to learn about French cuisine and wine
* Explore the city's many neighborhoods and districts, each with its own unique character and charm
* Don't forget to try some of the city's famous croissants, baguettes, and cheese!
