* Check-in at Hotel Eiffel Seine, a 4-star hotel with great views of the Eiffel Tower (approximately $150 per night)

**Day 2: Montmartre and the Latin Quarter**

* Start the day with a visit to the Sacré-Cœur Basilica in Montmartre, a historic neighborhood with narrow streets and charming cafes
* Explore the Latin Quarter, known for its narrow streets, charming cafes, and historic universities
* Visit the Luxembourg Gardens, a beautiful park with fountains, statues, and stunning views of the city
* In the evening, enjoy a Seine River cruise to see the city's landmarks illuminated at night

**Day 3: The Louvre and the Palais-Royal**

* Spend the morning visiting the Louvre Museum, one of the world's largest and most famous museums (approximately $18 per person)
* Visit the Palais-Royal, a former royal palace with beautiful gardens and contemporary art installations
* Explore the trendy Marais neighborhood, known for its fashionable boutiques, art galleries, and restaurants
* In the evening, enjoy a dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant, such as Le Grand Vefour

**Day 4: Versailles and the Champs-Élysées**

* Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles, a former royal palace with stunning gardens and fountain shows (approximately $20 per person, including transportation)
* Return to Paris and spend the afternoon shopping on the Champs-Élysées, one of the world's most famous avenues
* Visit the Arc de Triomphe, a monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France
* In the evening, enjoy a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge or Lido de Paris

**Day 5: Notre-Dame and the Île de la Cité**

* Spend the morning visiting Notre-Dame Cathedral, a beautiful and historic church that was built in the 12th century (free admission)
* Explore the Île de la Cité, a small island in the Seine River that's home to several historic landmarks, including the Sainte-Chapelle and the Conciergerie
* Visit the Musée d'Orsay, a museum that's home to an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art (approximately $12 per person)
* In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner at a classic Parisian bistro, such as Chez L'Ami Jean
