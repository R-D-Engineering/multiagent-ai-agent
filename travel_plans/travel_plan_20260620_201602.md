* Spend the afternoon exploring the city, starting from the iconic Eiffel Tower. Take a stroll along the Seine River and visit the nearby Champ de Mars park.

**Day 2: Museums and Galleries**

* Start the day with a visit to the famous Louvre Museum, home to the Mona Lisa and many other art treasures.
* Next, head to the Musée d'Orsay, which houses an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art.
* After lunch, visit the Musée Rodin, dedicated to the works of French sculptor Auguste Rodin.
* In the evening, enjoy a Seine River cruise, which offers a unique perspective on the city's landmarks and bridges.

**Day 3: Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur**

* Spend the morning exploring the charming neighborhood of Montmartre, known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city.
* Visit the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica, perched on a hill overlooking the city.
* Wander the narrow streets and alleys, discovering quaint cafes, shops, and historic buildings.
* In the evening, enjoy a cabaret show at the world-famous Moulin Rouge.

**Day 4: Palace of Versailles**

* Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles, a former royal residence with opulent decorations, stunning gardens, and impressive fountain shows.
* Visit the Hall of Mirrors, the Royal Chapel, and the Royal Opera House.
* Stroll through the beautiful gardens, which include the Orangery, the Lake, and the Grove of the Fauna.
* Return to Paris in the evening and enjoy a farewell dinner at a classic French restaurant.

**Day 5: Shopping and Departure**

* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or exploring a local market, such as the Marché aux Puces de Saint-Ouen (flea market).
* Visit the famous Galeries Lafayette or Le Bon Marché department stores for a unique shopping experience.
* Depart for the airport and your return flight, bringing back memories of your wonderful trip to Paris.
